# 1. Welcome to the Creator Club

<Callout title="Congratulations on joining the Creator Club!" emoji="tada">

This page will walk you through everything you need to know about being a creator—now and in the future.

# 2. Using the Creator Dashboard

Your Creator Dashboard is your central command. Here’s a brief overview of the sections you’ll find:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="list-check" title="Published Characters">
    View all your published AI characters, each with real-time performance metrics.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="graph-bar" title="Performance Trend">
    Keep track of trends in chats, interactions, and subscriber growth. Switch between 7-day,
    30-day, and 90-day views to monitor your progress.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="calendar-clock" title="Monthly Analytics">
    See aggregated stats, like total interactions, subscriber count, and more for the past 30 days.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="user" title="Profile & Settings">
    Edit your creator profile, add a bio, and customize how you appear to your audience.
    You can also update your profile picture or any personal details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

# 3. Creating and Publishing Characters

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="plus-circle" title="Creation Workflow">
    To create a new AI character, click the “+” or “New Character” button in your dashboard.
    You’ll be prompted to set attributes like name, description, avatar, and unique traits
    of your AI character.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="rocket" title="Publishing">
    Once you’ve refined your AI character, hit “Publish” to make it visible to the community.
    After publishing, check analytics in real time to see how users are interacting.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="gear" title="Managing Characters">
    <strong>Edit:</strong> Update any aspect of the character, from description to avatar.<br/>
    <strong>Start:</strong> Begin a live test or “pre-launch” chat session, so you can see how your
    character responds before sharing it widely.<br/>
    <strong>Share:</strong> Copy and share direct links, or embed on external platforms.<br/>
    <strong>Delete:</strong> Remove a character if you no longer want it on your profile.
    <Note>
      Once deleted, characters cannot be recovered.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

# 6. FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="help-circle" title="How many AI characters can I create?">
    We currently allow each creator to publish multiple characters, though there may be a cap
    in the future for performance reasons. Check your dashboard for any specific limitations.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="alert-triangle" title="Is there any restricted content?">
    We ask all creators to follow our Community Guidelines regarding prohibited or sensitive content.
    Make sure your AI characters comply with platform rules.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="external-link" title="Can I share my characters outside of mefriend.ai?">
    Yes! Use the “Share” option on any published character to get a direct link you can post on
    social media or embed on other websites.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="help-buoy" title="What if I need help or run into technical issues?">
    Visit our Support Page or join our Discord community for real-time assistance and tips from
    fellow creators.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
