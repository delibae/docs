# Creator Club Overview

A **creator** on mefriend.ai is a user who crafts AI characters for others to interact with. Creators gain access to special dashboards, metrics, and (soon) monetization tools. Whether you’re designing story-driven personalities or fun role-play companions, the Creator Club is here to support and spotlight your work.

## Creating & Publishing Characters

1. Click **New Character** and specify name, description, avatar, and unique traits.  
2. **Publish** to make it live. Track analytics in real time.  
3. **Edit**, **Test**, or **Delete** your character anytime.

<Callout title="Future Upgrades" emoji="rocket">
We plan to introduce advanced editor tools, allowing you to customize behaviors, set deeper personalities, and refine prompts with ease—everything to help your AI characters stand out.
</Callout>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="question-mark-circle" title="How many characters can I create?">
    You can currently publish multiple characters. In the future, there may be a cap for performance reasons, so keep an eye on your dashboard for updates.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="bolt" title="Are there any content restrictions?">
    Yes. We ask that all creators adhere to our Community Guidelines to ensure a safe environment. Restricted and sensitive content is not allowed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="arrow-top-right-on-square" title="Can I share my creations externally?">
    Absolutely. Use the “Share” option on your published character to get a direct link or embedded code for social media or other platforms.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="lifebuoy" title="Where do I go if I have technical issues?">
    Check our Support Page or join our Discord community for real-time assistance and tips. We’re here to help troubleshoot any hurdles you face.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
