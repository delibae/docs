## 2) Enhanced SFW Browsing Experience 🔒

Ever worried about browsing Mefriend.ai in public spaces like streets or cafés, concerned that NSFW content might unexpectedly pop up? 

We've got you covered! Our new blur feature discreetly hides NSFW images by default, ensuring comfortable browsing anywhere. And if you're in a private space —just one click instantly reveals everything clearly.

![Blurred NSFW Images](/images/changelog/2025_04_16/blurred.png)

We've enhanced our SFW mode by blurring NSFW images to respect your browsing preferences.

- **Safe Browsing**: NSFW characters aren't removed, just blurred for comfort.
