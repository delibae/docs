We're thrilled to announce our latest update designed to make your chats even more immersive and engaging! 🎉

## 1) Unlock Images 📸

<div align="center">
  <img src="/images/changelog/2025_04_01/unlock_1.png" alt="Unlock Images" />
</div>

We've introduced **Unlock Images**! 

Now, as you chat with your character, you'll gradually unlock stunning images that capture key moments—from that very first meeting ✨ to dramatic, script-worthy scenes 🎬

Chat freely and watch your interactions come alive with complimentary images that enhance your storytelling experience.

## 2) Track Relationships 💖

![track_relationships.png](/images/changelog/2025_04_01/relationship.png)

Deepen your connections with our new **Track Relationships** feature. 

As you chat more deeply with your character, your relationship level will increase through a visible score 📈. The higher the score, the stronger your connection becomes. It’s a simple way to track your bond over time, from first encounters 🤝 to deep friendships 🌟.

Prefer a more casual experience? No worries—this feature is fully optional and can be turned off anytime in your settings.

---

## 3) Web Notifications 🔔

![web_notifications.png](/images/changelog/2025_04_01/noti.png)

Stay up-to-date with the latest from Mefriend.ai! 

With **Web Notifications**, subscribe to receive real-time updates on status changes, upcoming events, and exclusive offers 🎁. Whether through our app or directly on the web, you'll never miss a beat.

---

## 4) Enhanced Memory for Your Character! 🧠✨


Experience richer conversations with our **Enhanced Memory** upgrade. We've significantly increased the memory limit for your characters so they can remember more of your interactions, leading to deeper, more personalized chats. Come in and enjoy the enhanced experience!
