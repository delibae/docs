We’ve rolled out a bunch of exciting updates! From daily free coins to a revamped home experience that learns from your chat activity, this release is all about making your journey smoother, more rewarding, and more fun. Check out the highlights below!

## 1) Daily Free Coins! Yes.

![Daily Bonus](/images/changelog/2025_03_10/daily_bonus.png)

Get daily free coins! Yes, you read that right—daily free coins are here!  
- **Easy to Claim**: Just tap the **coin button in the header** or visit the **Coin Detail Page** to grab your free coins each day. (Resets every 00:00 UTC)
- **No Strings Attached**: Stop by daily for free currency—use it anytime you like.

## 2) Revamped Home with Smarter Recommendations

![Revamped Home](/images/changelog/2025_03_10/revamped_home.png)

Your Home screen just got a major upgrade!  
- **Smarter Algorithm**: The more you chat, the better we learn what you like—so you’ll see characters you’ll love.  
- **Random Character Picks**: Tap the “Random” option on the home screen to get a fresh selection of **10 random characters** each time. Great for exploring new possibilities!

![Half Price Coin Sale](/images/changelog/2025_03_10/half_price.png)

We’re slashing coin prices by **up to 50%** for 2 days!

## 5) Mobile Install! (for Quicker Access)

![Install Mefriend to Home](/images/changelog/2025_03_10/install.png)

You can now install Mefriend.ai directly on your mobile home screen!  
- **One-Tap Launch**: Add the app icon to your home screen for instant access.

- **Easy Setup**: Just tap “Install” on your mobile device when prompted and follow the instructions.
- **More Native-Like Features Coming Soon**: We’re working on cool enhancements like push notifications through our new PWA approach—so install now to stay on the cutting edge!
