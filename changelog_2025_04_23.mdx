## 3) Lorebooks (Beta)

![Lorebook preview](/images/changelog/2025_04_23/lorebook_placeholder.png)

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="book-open" title="What’s a Lorebook?">
    A structured knowledge base—characters, places, items, events.  
    When keywords appear in chat, the model auto‑injects that lore.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="wand-magic-sparkles" title="How to Use">
    1. Open any character → **Customize** → pick your Lorebooks.  
    2. Start chatting; consistency handled.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="plus" title="Create & Share">
    **Create → Lorebook** to build your own. Publish privately or to the community library.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## 4) Scenarios (Beta)

![Scenario preview](/images/changelog/2025_04_23/scenario_placeholder.png)

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="film" title="What’s a Scenario?">
    A pre‑built setup—rooftop chase, cozy café, interstellar rescue.  
    Pair it with any character and jump right in.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="play-circle" title="How to Start">
    Same flow as Lorebooks: **Customize** → choose Scenarios → chat.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="pen" title="Build Your Own">
    **Create → Scenario** to script the opening, set the mood, and share it with others.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
