## 3) 📖 Lorebooks are now on!

Lorebooks are live!

![Lorebook preview](/images/changelog/2025_04_23/lorebook.png)

- **What’s a Lorebook?**  
  - A structured knowledge base—characters, places, items, events.  
  - Whenever a keyword appears in chat, the model pulls the matching lore.

- **How to Use**  
  1. Open any character → **Customize** → pick the Lorebooks you need.  
  2. Start chatting—consistency handled automatically.

- **Create & Share**  
  - **Create → Lorebook** to build your own.  
  - Publish privately or share it with the community library.

## 4) ✨ Scenarios to make chats more immersive!

Scenarios are live too!

![Scenario preview](/images/changelog/2025_04_23/scenario.png)

- **What’s a Scenario?**  
  - A pre‑built setup: rooftop chase, cozy café, interstellar rescue…  
  - Pair it with any character and dive straight in.

- **How to Start**  
  1. Open any character → **Customize** → pick your Scenarios.  
  2. Hit **Start Chat** and go.

- **Build Your Own**  
  - **Create → Scenario** to script the opener, set the mood, and share it with others.
