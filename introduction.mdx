# Introduction

<Callout title="Hello and welcome!" emoji="wave">
We’re thrilled to have you here. **Mefriend** is your home for creating and interacting with AI characters. This documentation will guide you through every step of your journey—from designing unique AI personalities to engaging with an ever-growing community.
</Callout>

Mefriend is designed to give you the flexibility to:
- **Create** AI Characters with custom traits and abilities.
- **Share** your creations with friends and the broader community.
- **Collaborate** with other creators to refine and innovate on character ideas.
- **Engage** in meaningful conversations with AI characters, no matter their style or specialty.

<Note>
We’re constantly evolving—keep an eye on these docs for new features, best practices, and creative tips!
</Note>

## What You’ll Find Here
- **Creator Guides:** Step-by-step instructions for building, publishing, and promoting your AI characters.
- **Platform Essentials:** Learn how to navigate the dashboard, understand key metrics, and manage settings.
- **Tips & Tricks:** Helpful pointers to maximize your experience and make your AI characters stand out.
